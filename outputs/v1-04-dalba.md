### **Korean Skincare Ingredients Explained (Translated & Described for International Users)**  

Below is a detailed breakdown of the ingredients, including **translation, explanation, skin type cautions, and environmental flags**:  

---

### **1. Water (정제수)**  
- **Explanation**: Purified water, a base in most skincare products.  
- **Skin Caution**: Generally safe.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Low concern.  

### **2. Propanediol (프로판다이올)**  
- **Explanation**: A natural humectant (moisturizer) from plant sources.  
- **Skin Caution**: Safe for all skin types.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Biodegradable, eco-friendly.  

### **3. Butyloctyl Salicylate (부틸옥틸살리실레이트)**  
- **Explanation**: A UV-blocking agent (sunscreen booster) and skin conditioner.  
- **Skin Caution**: May cause mild irritation in sensitive skin.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Low toxicity, but not fully biodegradable.  

### **4. Dicaprylyl Carbonate (다이카프릴릴카보네이트)**  
- **Explanation**: A lightweight emollient (softens skin) and solvent.  
- **Skin Caution**: Generally non-irritating.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Low concern.  

### **5. Titanium Dioxide (티타늄디옥사이드)**  
- **Explanation**: A physical sunscreen (mineral UV filter) and whitening agent.  
- **Skin Caution**: Can leave a white cast; avoid if sensitive to minerals.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Some nano-particles may harm marine life.  

### **6. Diisopropyl Sebacate (다이아이소프로필세바케이트)**  
- **Explanation**: A softening agent and emollient.  
- **Skin Caution**: Safe for most skin types.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Low toxicity.  

### **7. 2,3-Butanediol (2,3-부탄다이올)**  
- **Explanation**: A moisturizer and solvent derived from fermentation.  
- **Skin Caution**: Gentle and non-irritating.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Biodegradable.  

### **8. Diethylamino Hydroxybenzoyl Hexyl Benzoate (디에칠아미노하이드록시벤조일헥실벤조에이트)**  
- **Explanation**: A chemical UV absorber (broad-spectrum sunscreen).  
- **Skin Caution**: May irritate sensitive skin; avoid if prone to allergies.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Not fully biodegradable.  

### **9. Polysilicone-15 (폴리실리콘-15)**  
- **Explanation**: A silicone-based film former and sunscreen booster.  
- **Skin Caution**: Can clog pores in oily/acne-prone skin.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Not eco-friendly (silicone buildup in waterways).  

### **10. Niacinamide (나이아신아마이드)**  
- **Explanation**: A vitamin B3 derivative that brightens, reduces inflammation, and strengthens the skin barrier.  
- **Skin Caution**: Safe for all skin types.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Low concern.  

### **11. Dibutyl Adipate (다이부틸아디페이트)**  
- **Explanation**: A softening agent and emollient.  
- **Skin Caution**: Generally non-irritating.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Low toxicity.  

### **12. Ethylhexyl Triazone (에칠헥실트리아존)**  
- **Explanation**: A UV filter (sunscreen) that stabilizes other filters.  
- **Skin Caution**: May cause reactions in sensitive skin.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Not fully biodegradable.  

### **13. 1,2-Hexanediol (1,2-헥산다이올)**  
- **Explanation**: A preservative booster and moisturizer.  
- **Skin Caution**: Mildly irritating for very sensitive skin.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Low toxicity.  

### **14–25. Plant Extracts (흰서양송로추출물 ~ 병풀추출물)**  
- **Explanation**: Various herbal extracts (truffle, ivy fruit, purslane, borage, etc.) with antioxidant and soothing properties.  
- **Skin Caution**: Rare allergies possible (patch-test recommended).  
- **Environmental Flag**: Sustainable if ethically sourced.  

### **26–29. Essential Oils (오렌지오일 ~ 라벤더꽃추출물)**  
- **Explanation**: Aromatic oils (orange, jojoba, tangerine, etc.) for fragrance and skin benefits.  
- **Skin Caution**: Can irritate sensitive/acne-prone skin. Avoid if prone to reactions.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Some are ecologically sustainable.  

### **30. Ethylhexylglycerin (에칠헥실글리세린)**  
- **Explanation**: A preservative and skin conditioner.  
- **Skin Caution**: Generally safe.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Low concern.  

### **31–45. Miscellaneous Functionals (펜틸렌글라이콜 ~ 트라이에톡시카프릴릴실레인)**  
- **Explanation**: Includes moisturizers (glycerin), emulsifiers (sucrose stearate), thickeners (xanthan gum), and silicone-derived ingredients.  
- **Skin Caution**: Most are non-irritating; silicones may clog pores.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Varies (silicones and synthetic polymers are less eco-friendly).  

### **46. Adenosine (아데노신)**  
- **Explanation**: An anti-aging compound that boosts skin repair.  
- **Skin Caution**: Safe for all skin types.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Low concern.  

### **47. Squalane (스쿠알란)**  
- **Explanation**: A moisturizing oil derived from olives or sugarcane.  
- **Skin Caution**: Lightweight and non-comedogenic.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Sustainable if plant-derived.  

### **48–55. Active Ingredients (락토바실러스발효물 ~ 포도주)**  
- **Explanation**: Includes fermented extracts (probiotic benefits), antioxidants (glutathione), and hydrators (hyaluronic acid).  
- **Skin Caution**: Rare sensitivities; patch-test fermented ingredients.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Generally eco-friendly.  

### **56. Ultramarines (울트라마린)**  
- **Explanation**: Synthetic pigment for color.  
- **Skin Caution**: Generally safe, but may stain light fabrics.  
- **Environmental Flag**: Low concern.  

### **57–58. Fragrance Components (리모넨, 시트랄)**  
- **Explanation**: Aromatic compounds from essential oils.  
- **Skin Caution**: **High risk for sensitive skin** (can cause redness, itching).  
- **Environmental Flag**: Some are derived from sustainable sources.  

---

### **Key Cautions for Sensitive Skin**  
- **Avoid/Use Caution**: Titanium Dioxide, Silicones, Fragrance (Limonene, Citral), and certain plant extracts.  
- **Eco-Conscious Swaps**: Opt for mineral sunscreens (zinc oxide) and silicone-free formulas.
