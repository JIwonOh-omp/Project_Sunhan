Here’s a breakdown of the ingredients in your Korean cosmetic product, translated and explained for international users. Each section includes **translation, explanation, skin type cautions**, and **environmental flags**:

---

### **1. Pomegranate Seed Oil (석류씨오일)**  
- **Explanation**: Rich in antioxidants and fatty acids; nourishes skin and protects against free radicals.  
- **Skin Caution**: Generally safe, but may cause irritation in sensitive skin.  
- **Environment**: Sustainably sourced if organic; biodegradable.  

### **2. Black Mulberry Fruit Extract (검은뽕나무열매추출물)**  
- **Explanation**: High in vitamins C & K; brightens skin and reduces inflammation.  
- **Skin Caution**: Low risk, but rare allergic reactions possible.  
- **Environment**: Plant-derived and biodegradable.  

### **3. Western Strawberry Leaf Extract (서양산딸기잎추출물)**  
- **Explanation**: Soothes skin with anti-inflammatory properties.  
- **Skin Caution**: Safe for most types; patch-test if prone to allergies.  
- **Environment**: Eco-friendly botanical ingredient.  

### **4. Homosalate (호모살레이트)**  
- **Explanation**: UVB chemical sunscreen filter.  
- **Skin Caution**: May irritate sensitive skin; avoid if prone to breakouts.  
- **Environment**: ⚠️ **Reef-harming** (oxybenzone-free but still not ocean-safe).  

### **5. Aloe Vera Leaf Juice (알로에베라잎즙)**  
- **Explanation**: Hydrates and calms irritated skin.  
- **Skin Caution**: Rarely causes allergies; avoid if sensitive to lilies.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable and renewable.  

### **6. Jojoba Seed Oil (호호바씨오일)**  
- **Explanation**: Moisturizes without clogging pores (similar to skin’s natural oils).  
- **Skin Caution**: Suitable for most types, including acne-prone.  
- **Environment**: Sustainable if non-GMO.  

### **7. Black Tea Extract (홍차추출물)**  
- **Explanation**: Antioxidant-rich; reduces redness and puffiness.  
- **Skin Caution**: Caffeine may dry out very dry skin.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

### **8. Shea Butter (시어버터)**  
- **Explanation**: Deeply hydrates and softens skin.  
- **Skin Caution**: May feel heavy for oily skin; comedogenic for some.  
- **Environment**: Ethical sourcing varies (look for fair-trade).  

### **9. Oat Kernel Extract (귀리커넬추출물)**  
- **Explanation**: Soothes eczema and sensitive skin; anti-inflammatory.  
- **Skin Caution**: Rare gluten traces (avoid if celiac).  
- **Environment**: Renewable and biodegradable.  

### **10. Ethylhexyl Salicylate (에칠헥실살리실레이트)**  
- **Explanation**: UVB sunscreen filter; less irritating than oxybenzone.  
- **Skin Caution**: Generally mild but can cause breakouts.  
- **Environment**: ⚠️ **Moderately harmful** to marine life in high concentrations.  

### **11. Benzophenone-3 (벤조페논-3)**  
- **Explanation**: UVA/UVB filter; increases sun protection.  
- **Skin Caution**: Can cause allergies or hormone disruption (avoid if sensitive).  
- **Environment**: ⚠️ **Harmful to corals**—avoid for reef-safe formulas.  

### **12. Avobenzone (부틸메톡시디벤조일메탄)**  
- **Explanation**: Broad-spectrum UVA protector.  
- **Skin Caution**: May irritate sensitive skin; unstable without stabilizers.  
- **Environment**: ⚠️ **Reef-toxic**—avoid for ocean-safe products.  

### **13. Glycerin (글리세린)**  
- **Explanation**: Humectant that draws moisture into skin.  
- **Skin Caution**: Safe for all types; may initially draw moisture from dry skin.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

### **14. Octocrylene (옥토크릴렌)**  
- **Explanation**: UVB stabilizer in sunscreens; reduces irritation.  
- **Skin Caution**: Low risk but may accumulate in body over time.  
- **Environment**: ⚠️ **Potential reef harm** in high doses.  

### **15. Dimethicone (다이메티콘)**  
- **Explanation**: Silicone that smooths skin texture.  
- **Skin Caution**: Can clog pores; avoid if acne-prone.  
- **Environment**: ❌ **Not biodegradable**—persists in waterways.  

### **16. Panthenol (판테놀)**  
- **Explanation**: Pro-vitamin B5; hydrates and repairs skin barrier.  
- **Skin Caution**: Safe for all types.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

### **17. Caprylic/Capric Triglycerides (카프릴릭/카프릭트라이글리세라이드)**  
- **Explanation**: Lightweight moisturizer from coconut/palm oil.  
- **Skin Caution**: Non-comedogenic; suits oily skin.  
- **Environment**: Sustainable if RSPO-certified palm-derived.  

### **18. Squalane (스쿠알란)**  
- **Explanation**: Mimics skin’s natural oils; boosts hydration.  
- **Skin Caution**: Suitable for all types.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable (better if plant-derived).  

### **19. Cetyl Alcohol (세틸알코올)**  
- **Explanation**: Fatty alcohol that thickens textures; non-drying.  
- **Skin Caution**: Low comedogenicity.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

### **20. Cetearyl Alcohol (세테아릴알코올)**  
- **Explanation**: Emulsifier for creamy formulas.  
- **Skin Caution**: Generally non-irritating.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

### **21. Tocopheryl Acetate (토코페릴아세테이트)**  
- **Explanation**: Stable form of vitamin E; antioxidant protection.  
- **Skin Caution**: Rare allergies.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

### **22. Glyceryl Stearate (글리세릴스테아레이트)**  
- **Explanation**: Emulsifier and moisturizer.  
- **Skin Caution**: Low risk.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

### **23. Licorice Root Extract (스페인감초뿌리추출물)**  
- **Explanation**: Brightens dark spots and reduces inflammation.  
- **Skin Caution**: Avoid if allergic to legumes.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

### **24. Ethylhexylglycerin (에틸헥실글리세린)**  
- **Explanation**: Preservative booster; enhances antimicrobial effects.  
- **Skin Caution**: Mild irritant for sensitive skin.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

### **25. Caprylyl Glycol (카프릴릴글라이콜)**  
- **Explanation**: Humectant and mild preservative.  
- **Skin Caution**: Low sensitivity.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

### **26. Sodium Hyaluronate (소듐하이알루로네이트)**  
- **Explanation**: Hydrating form of hyaluronic acid.  
- **Skin Caution**: Safe for all types.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

### **27. Phenoxyethanol (페녹시에탄올)**  
- **Explanation**: Common preservative.  
- **Skin Caution**: ⚠️ **Avoid in high concentrations** (EU limits at 1%); may irritate babies.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable but controversial in natural cosmetics.  

### **28. Hydrolyzed DNA (하이드롤라이즈드디엔에이)**  
- **Explanation**: Anti-aging ingredient from salmon DNA.  
- **Skin Caution**: ⚠️ **Avoid if vegan/vegetarian** or fish-allergic.  
- **Environment**: Non-vegan source.  

### **29. Sodium Benzoate (소듐벤조에이트)**  
- **Explanation**: Preservative that inhibits mold/bacteria.  
- **Skin Caution**: ⚠️ **Avoid with vitamin C** (forms benzene). Irritating for some.  
- **Environment**: Biodegradable.  

---

### **Key Warnings**:  
- **Reef-Harming Ingredients**: Homosalate, Benzophenone-3, Octocrylene, Avobenzone.  
- **Sensitive Skin Triggers**: Phenoxyethanol, Dimethicone, Benzophenone-3.  
- **Non-Vegan**: Hydrolyzed DNA (from salmon).
