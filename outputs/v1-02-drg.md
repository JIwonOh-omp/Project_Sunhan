Here’s the detailed breakdown of the skincare ingredient list for international users, translated and explained with key details:

---

### **1. Purified Water (정제수)**  
- **Translation**: Purified Water  
- **Explanation**: A base ingredient that provides hydration and dissolves other soluble components.  
- **Skin Type Caution**: Generally safe for all skin types.  
- **Environmental Flag**: ✅ Eco-friendly (no specific concerns).  

---

### **2. Cyclopentasiloxane (사이클로펜타실록세인)**  
- **Translation**: Cyclopentasiloxane  
- **Explanation**: A silicone-based emollient that smooths skin texture and enhances spreadability.  
- **Skin Type Caution**: May clog pores for acne-prone/oily skin. Avoid if sensitive to silicones.  
- **Environmental Flag**: ⚠️ Some environmental groups discourage due to bioaccumulation concerns.  

---

### **3. Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate (에칠헥실메톡시신나메이트)**  
- **Translation**: Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate (Octinoxate)  
- **Explanation**: UVB chemical filter that protects against sunburn.  
- **Skin Type Caution**: Can cause irritation for sensitive skin.  
- **Environmental Flag**: ❌ Banned in Hawaii (harms coral reefs). Seek reef-safe alternatives if eco-conscious.  

---

### **4. Zinc Oxide (징크옥사이드, CI 77947)**  
- **Translation**: Zinc Oxide  
- **Explanation**: Mineral UV filter that reflects both UVA/UVB rays. Gentle for sensitive skin.  
- **Skin Type Caution**: Safe for all skin types, including sensitive/acne-prone.  
- **Environmental Flag**: ✅ Reef-safe and biodegradable.  

---

### **5. Cyclohexasiloxane (사이클로헥사실록세인)**  
- **Translation**: Cyclohexasiloxane  
- **Explanation**: Lightweight silicone that improves product texture and absorption.  
- **Skin Type Caution**: May feel heavy for oily skin.  
- **Environmental Flag**: ⚠️ Less eco-friendly than plant-based alternatives.  

---

### **6. Ethylhexyl Salicylate (에칠헥실살리실레이트)**  
- **Translation**: Ethylhexyl Salicylate (Octisalate)  
- **Explanation**: UVB absorber that stabilizes other sunscreen ingredients.  
- **Skin Type Caution**: Low irritation risk, but avoid if allergic to salicylates.  
- **Environmental Flag**: ⚠️ Limited reef safety data; moderate concern.  

---

### **7. Butylene Glycol (부틸렌글라이콜)**  
- **Translation**: Butylene Glycol  
- **Explanation**: Humectant that retains moisture and enhances penetration of actives.  
- **Skin Type Caution**: Generally safe, but rare irritation possible.  
- **Environmental Flag**: ✅ Low environmental impact.  

---

### **8. Methyl Methacrylate Crosspolymer (메틸메타크릴레이트크로스폴리머)**  
- **Translation**: Methyl Methacrylate Crosspolymer  
- **Explanation**: Film-forming agent that improves longevity of makeup/formula.  
- **Skin Type Caution**: Avoid if prone to dryness (may feel tight).  
- **Environmental Flag**: ✅ No major concerns.  

---

### **9. Polyglyceryl-3 Polydimethylsiloxyethyl Dimethicone (폴리글리세릴-3폴리다이메틸실록시에틸다이메티콘)**  
- **Translation**: Polyglyceryl-3 Polydimethylsiloxyethyl Dimethicone  
- **Explanation**: Silicone emulsifier that creates a smooth, long-lasting finish.  
- **Skin Type Caution**: Heavy for oily/combination skin.  
- **Environmental Flag**: ⚠️ Synthetic silicone with moderate eco-impact.  

---

### **10. Titanium Dioxide (티타늄디옥사이드, CI 77891)**  
- **Translation**: Titanium Dioxide  
- **Explanation**: Physical sunscreen that blocks UVA/UVB rays. Often used in mineral makeup.  
- **Skin Type Caution**: Safe for sensitive skin; may leave a white cast.  
- **Environmental Flag**: ✅ Reef-safe in non-nano form.  

---

### **Key Actives & Natural Extracts**  
- **Niacinamide (나이아신아마이드)**: Brightens skin, reduces pores, soothes irritation. Safe for all skin types.  
- **Grape Seed Oil (포도씨오일)**: Antioxidant-rich, hydrating. Avoid if allergic to seeds.  
- **Green Tea Extract (녹차추출물)**: Anti-inflammatory, suitable for acne/rosacea.  

---

### **Preservatives & Functional Additives**  
- **1,2-Hexanediol (1,2-헥산다이올)**: Mild preservative with antimicrobial properties.  
- **Caprylyl Glycol (카프릴릴글라이콜)**: Humectant and preservative booster.  
- **Phenoxyethanol (다이소듐이디티에이)**: Common preservative; controversial in high doses (here, safe).  

---

### **Colorants**  
- **Yellow/Red Iron Oxides (황색/적색산화철)**: Natural pigments for tinting. Safe for most skin types.  

---

### **Eco-Friendly Alternatives to Consider**  
Replace silicones (e.g., cyclopentasiloxane) with squalane or plant oils. Opt for non-nano zinc oxide/titanium dioxide for reef safety.
