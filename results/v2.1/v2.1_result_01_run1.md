### 2. **성분 해설**  
1. **Punica Granatum Seed Oil** – Rich in antioxidants and fatty acids, known for moisturizing and anti-inflammatory properties.  
2. **Morus Nigra Fruit Extract** – Contains polyphenols and vitamins, often used for brightening and antioxidant benefits.  
3. **Rubus Idaeus (Raspberry) Leaf Extract** – Astringent properties, used for soothing and toning skin.  
4. **Homosalate** – UVB absorber used in sunscreens; may raise environmental concerns at high concentrations.  
5. **Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice** – Soothing and hydrating, commonly used for calming irritated skin.  
6. **Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil** – Mimics skin’s natural oils, provides lightweight moisturization.  
7. **Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract** – Antioxidant-rich green tea extract, helps protect against free radicals.  
8. **Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter** – Deeply nourishing and emollient, used for dry skin.  
9. **Avena Sativa (Oat) Kernel Extract** – Soothes sensitive or itchy skin, provides gentle hydration.  
10. **Ethylhexyl Salicylate** – UVB filter in sunscreens, also used as a fragrance ingredient.  
11. **Benzophenone-3 (Oxybenzone)** – Broad-spectrum UV filter; banned in Hawaii for sunscreen due to coral reef concerns.  
12. **Butyl Methoxydibenzoylmethane (Avobenzone)** – UVA filter in sunscreens; linked to coral reef harm in studies.  
13. **Glycerin** – Humectant that attracts moisture to the skin.  
14. **Octocrylene** – UVB absorber; banned in Palau for sunscreen.  
15. **Dimethicone** – Silicone-based ingredient that smooths skin texture and provides a protective barrier.  
16. **Panthenol** – Vitamin B5 derivative, helps retain moisture and supports skin barrier.  
17. **Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride** – Lightweight emollient derived from coconut and glycerin.  
18. **Squalane** – Skin-identical oil that provides deep hydration without greasiness.  
19. **Cetyl Alcohol** – Fatty alcohol used as a texture enhancer and emulsifier (non-drying).  
20. **Cetearyl Alcohol** – Fatty alcohol that thickens formulas and improves absorption.  
21. **Tocopheryl Acetate** – Stabilized form of vitamin E, an antioxidant that protects skin.  
22. **Glyceryl Stearate** – Emulsifier that helps mix water and oil-based ingredients.  
23. **Glycyrrhiza Glabra (Licorice) Root Extract** – Anti-inflammatory and brightening agent.  
24. **Ethylhexylglycerin** – Preservative booster with mild antimicrobial properties.  
25. **Caprylyl Glycol** – Humectant and preservative enhancer.  
26. **Sodium Hyaluronate** – Low-molecular-weight hyaluronic acid for intense hydration.  
27. **Phenoxyethanol** – Common preservative with mild antimicrobial properties.  
28. **Hydrolyzed DNA** – Skin-conditioning agent derived from fish DNA.  
29. **Sodium Benzoate** – Preservative that inhibits microbial growth.

### 3. **피부타입 주의**  
- **Sensitive Skin**  
  - Contains *Phenoxyethanol* (preservative) and *Fragrance* (if present in hidden ingredients). Patch test recommended.  
  - Contains **EU-listed fragrance allergen** (e.g., *Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract* may contain volatile compounds).  
- **Dry Skin**  
  - *Dimethicone* and fatty alcohols (*Cetyl Alcohol*, *Cetearyl Alcohol*) provide occlusive hydration but may feel heavy.  
  - *Phenoxyethanol* and *Sodium Benzoate* are preservatives that may irritate dry, compromised barriers.

### 4. **환경 플래그**  
- **Tier 1 (Legal Bans)**  
  - **Benzophenone-3 (Oxybenzone)** – Banned in Hawaii for sunscreen (facial-cosmetic exemption applies).  
  - **Octocrylene** – Banned in Palau for sunscreen.  
- **Tier 2 (Research Concerns)**  
  - **Homosalate** – Studies suggest potential harm to coral reefs at high concentrations.  
  - **Butyl Methoxydibenzoylmethane (Avobenzone)** – Linked to coral bleaching in lab studies.  
  - **Ethylhexyl Salicylate** – Some studies raise concerns about marine toxicity.  

--- 

**Final Note**: This product contains sunscreen filters with environmental concerns. For reef-safe alternatives, consider mineral-based sunscreens (e.g., zinc oxide, titanium dioxide).
