### (2) 성분 해설  
1. *Punica Granatum Seed Oil*: Rich in antioxidants, moisturizes skin.  
2. *Morus Nigra Fruit Extract*: Extract from black mulberry fruit, used for its soothing properties.  
3. *Rubus Idaeus (Raspberry) Leaf Extract*: Contains vitamins and minerals, known for its astringent effects.  
4. *Homosalate*: UVB sunscreen agent, helps protect skin from sunburn.  
5. *Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice*: Soothes and hydrates skin, known for calming irritation.  
6. *Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil*: Mimics skin’s natural oils, provides lightweight moisture.  
7. *Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract*: Antioxidant-rich green tea extract, may protect against environmental stressors.  
8. *Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter*: Deeply nourishing butter, ideal for dry skin.  
9. *Avena Sativa (Oat) Kernel Extract*: Calms sensitive skin with anti-inflammatory properties.  
10. *Ethylhexyl Salicylate*: UV filter that helps prevent sun damage.  
11. *Benzophenone-3 (Oxybenzone)*: UVB/UVA sunscreen agent, may cause sensitivity in some.  
12. *Butyl Methoxydibenzoylmethane (Avobenzone)*: Broad-spectrum UVA protection, can degrade without stabilizers.  
13. *Glycerin*: Humectant that draws moisture into the skin.  
14. *Octocrylene*: UVB filter that stabilizes other sunscreen ingredients.  
15. *Dimethicone*: Silicone-based ingredient that smooths skin texture.  
16. *Panthenol*: Vitamin B5 derivative, strengthens skin barrier.  
17. *Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride*: Lightweight oil from coconut/fractions, moisturizes without greasiness.  
18. *Squalane*: Natural emollient derived from olives, mimics skin’s sebum.  
19. *Cetyl Alcohol*: Fatty alcohol that thickens formulas (non-irritating).  
20. *Cetearyl Alcohol*: Emulsifier and thickener, improves texture of creams.  
21. *Tocopheryl Acetate*: Stable form of vitamin E, antioxidant protection.  
22. *Glyceryl Stearate*: Emulsifier that helps blend water and oil.  
23. *Glycyrrhiza Glabra (Licorice) Root Extract*: Brightening agent with anti-inflammatory benefits.  
24. *Ethylhexylglycerin*: Preservative booster and skin-conditioning agent.  
25. *Caprylyl Glycol*: Humectant and mild preservative.  
26. *Sodium Hyaluronate*: Hydrating form of hyaluronic acid, retains moisture.  
27. *Phenoxyethanol*: Common preservative, effective against microbes.  
28. *Hydrolyzed DNA*: Moisturizing agent derived from animal or plant DNA.  
29. *Sodium Benzoate*: Preservative that inhibits bacterial growth.

### (3) 피부타입 주의  
- **민감성 (Sensitive)**:  
  - Contains fragrance allergens (*Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract* — potential allergen; *Phenoxyethanol* — mild irritant for some). Patch test recommended.  
  - Contains sunscreen agents (*Oxybenzone*, *Avobenzone*, *Homosalate*, *Octocrylene*) — may cause reactions in sensitive skin.  
- **건성 (Dry)**:  
  - Contains humectants (*Glycerin*, *Sodium Hyaluronate*) and emollients (*Shea Butter*, *Jojoba Oil*) — suitable for dry skin.  
  - *Cetyl Alcohol* and *Cetearyl Alcohol* are non-drying fatty alcohols.  
- **지성/여드름성 (Oily/Acne-Prone)**:  
  - *Dimethicone* and *Squalane* are lightweight oils, less likely to clog pores.  
  - *Ethylhexyl Salicylate* is a non-comedogenic UV filter.

### (4) 환경 플래그  
- **산호초 유해 성분 (Coral Reef)**:  
  - *Benzophenone-3 (Oxybenzone)*: Banned in sunscreen sold in Hawaii (with facial-cosmetic exemption).  
  - *Octocrylene*: Banned in Palau.  
  - *Homosalate* and *Butyl Methoxydibenzoylmethane (Avobenzone)*: Studies have raised concerns about coral reef effects, but not legally banned.
