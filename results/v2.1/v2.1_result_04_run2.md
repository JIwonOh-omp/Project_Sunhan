#### **(2) Ingredient Explanations**  
- **Water, Glycerin, Butylene Glycol** – Hydrating and solvent agents.  
- **Niacinamide, Panthenol, Adenosine, Glutathione, Allantoin** – Skin-brightening, soothing, and anti-aging actives.  
- **Titanium Dioxide, Polysilicone-15, Ethylhexyl Triazone, Diethylamino Hydroxybenzoyl Hexyl Benzoate** – UV filters (sunscreen).  
- **Jojoba Oil, Squalane, Tocopherol** – Moisturizing and antioxidant lipids.  
- **Centella Asiatica Extract, Houttuynia Cordata Extract** – Calming botanical extracts.  
- **Xanthan Gum, Acrylates Copolymer** – Texture-modifying thickeners.  
- **Limonene, Citral** – Fragrance components (potential allergens).  
- **Lactobacillus Ferment, Biosaccharide Gum-1** – Ferment-derived skin-conditioning agents.  
- **Hydrolyzed Hyaluronic Acid** – Low-molecular-weight hydrator.  

---

#### **(3) Skin Type Notes**  
- **Sensitive Skin**:  
  - Contains **Limonene, Citral** (EU-listed fragrance allergens). Patch test recommended.  
  - Contains **essential oils** (e.g., Lavandula Angustifolia, Citrus Aurantium Dulcis). Patch test first if sensitive.  
- **Dry Skin**:  
  - High concentration of humectants (Glycerin, Butylene Glycol, Panthenol) and emollients (Squalane, Jojoba Oil).  
  - No drying alcohols (e.g., Denatured Alcohol).  
- **Oily/Acne-Prone Skin**:  
  - Contains **Butyloctyl Salicylate** (non-comedogenic emollient).  
  - Avoid if sensitive to fragrance or essential oils.
