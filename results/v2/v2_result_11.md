### 3. Skin‑Type Cautions

- **Sensitive skin**  
  - Contains **Fragrance (Parfum)** – a common irritant. Patch test recommended.  
  - Contains **Limonene**, **Citronellol**, **Geraniol**, **Citral**, **Linalool** – fragrance allergens that may cause irritation. Patch test recommended for sensitive skin.  

- **Dry skin**  
  - **Denatured alcohol** is not present, so no drying effect.  
  - No sulfate cleansers (SLS/SLES) are listed.  

- **Acne‑prone / Oily skin**  
  - No comedogenic‑related warnings are provided (the system does not issue comedogenicity alerts).  

- **General**  
  - **Salicylic Acid** and **Capryloyl Salicylic Acid** are BHA exfoliants; they may cause mild irritation, especially at higher concentrations. Use with caution if you have very sensitive skin.
