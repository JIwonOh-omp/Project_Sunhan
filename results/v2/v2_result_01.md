### Ingredient Descriptions (one line per ingredient)

1. **Punica Granatum Seed Oil** – moisturizing oil rich in fatty acids and antioxidants.  
2. **Morus Nigra Fruit Extract** – antioxidant‑rich fruit extract that may help brighten skin.  
3. **Rubus Fruticosus Leaf Extract** – leaf extract providing mild antioxidant and soothing properties.  
4. **Homosalate** – UV‑filter that absorbs UVB rays.  
5. **Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice** – soothing, hydrating juice from aloe leaves.  
6. **Simmondsia Chinensis Seed Oil** – lightweight, non‑greasy oil that helps retain moisture.  
7. **Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract** – antioxidant‑rich tea extract that can protect against free radicals.  
8. **Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter** – emollient butter that softens and protects the skin barrier.  
9. **Avena Sativa (Oat) Kernel Extract** – oat extract known for calming and relieving irritation.  
10. **Ethylhexyl Salicylate** – UV‑filter that absorbs UVB; not a BHA.  
11. **Oxybenzone (Benzophenone‑3)** – UV‑filter that absorbs UVB and some UVA.  
12. **Avobenzone (Butyl Methoxydibenzoylmethane)** – broad‑spectrum UVA filter.  
13. **Glycerin** – humectant that draws water into the skin.  
14. **Octocrylene** – UV‑filter that absorbs UVB and stabilizes other filters.  
15. **Dimethicone** – silicone that provides a smooth, silky feel and reduces friction.  
16. **Panthenol** – provitamin B5 that hydrates and improves skin elasticity.  
17. **Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride** – emollient ester that helps dissolve oils and provides a light texture.  
18. **Squalane** – skin‑compatible oil that mimics natural sebum and adds softness.  
19. **Cetyl Alcohol** – fatty alcohol used as an emollient and thickener; can be drying if high in the list.  
20. **Cetearyl Alcohol** – fatty alcohol that acts as an emollient and stabilizer; generally non‑irritating.  
21. **Tocopheryl Acetate** – vitamin E derivative that offers antioxidant protection.  
22. **Glyceryl Stearate** – emulsifier and mild moisturizer derived from glycerin and stearic acid.  
23. **Glycyrrhiza Glabra (Licorice) Root Extract** – extract that may help reduce redness and pigmentation.  
24. **Ethylhexyl Glycinate** – emollient that improves skin feel and provides mild UV protection.  
25. **Caprylyl Glycol** – multifunctional preservative and moisturizer.  
26. **Sodium Hyaluronate** – low‑molecular‑weight hyaluronic acid that deeply hydrates.  
27. **Phenoxyethanol** – common preservative; generally well‑tolerated but can cause irritation in sensitive skin.  
28. **Hydrolyzed DNA** – ingredient that may support skin repair and moisture retention.

### Environmental Flags

- **Tier 1 (legal bans)** – **Oxybenzone (Benzophenone‑3)**, **Octinoxate (Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate)**, **Octocrylene** are banned in Hawaii (with a facial‑cosmetic exemption) and prohibited in Palau.  
- **Tier 2 (concern raised)** – **Homosalate** and **Avobenzone (Butyl Methoxydibenzoylmethane)** have been flagged in some studies for potential effects on coral reefs, though they are not legally banned.  

*No other ingredients in the list carry environmental warnings under the current regulations.*
